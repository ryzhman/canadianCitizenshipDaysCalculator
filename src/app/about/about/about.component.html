Service features

DaysBefore.ca helps you to calculate the date when you can apply for Canadian citizenship.

As of now, a candidate is required to reside in Canada for 1035 days before applying for a citizenship.

However, all the trips abroad must be subtracted from the number of days of your stay.

This service will help you to calculate when you can apply for Canadian citizenship including all of your trips outside Canada.

How to use the service

  1. Enter the landing date
  2. Enter the information about your trips abroad
  3. Get the date when to apply for Canadian citizenship
